    Services offered

    A session with your preferred specialist where they will conduct a comprehensive health assessment, collect a full medical history, and build a personalized care plan that works to suit your needs and address any concerns you may be having.

    Estimated duration: 45-60 mins

    Price: $40/session

    Massage Therapy consists primarily of hands-on manipulation of the soft tissues of the body, specifically, the muscles, connective tissue, tendons, ligaments and joints for the purpose of optimizing health.

    Estimated duration: 60 mins

    Price: $50/session

    Acupuncture is the practice of penetrating the skin with thin, solid, metallic needles which are then activated through gentle and specific movements of the practitioner's hands or with electrical stimulation.

    Estimated duration: 60 mins

    Price: $60/session

    Our Physiotherapist can help to treat your TMJ by teaching you relaxation, stretching and strengthening exercises for the face, head and neck muscles. These exercises can also help to augment the effect of the appliance or mouth guard your dentist may have already provided to you. The specialist will work with you to construct a personalized program that meets your needs.

    Estimated duration: 60 mins

    Price: $40/session

    Custom knee braces are custom-fitted to your knee to help provide the right fit and protection for your knee. Our custom knee braces are low-profile and light-weight allowing you to stay active without the bulk. These knee braces are ideal for many sports like football, basketball, football, volleyball, and water sports.

    Estimated duration: 40-60 mins

    Price: $150 (including assessment costs)

    Vestibular rehabilitation therapy (VRT) is a specific form of physical therapy designed to habituate symptoms, and promote adaptation to and substitution for various aspects of deficits related to a wide variety of balance disorders.

    Estimated duration: 50-60 mins

    Price: $55/session
